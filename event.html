About Handling Events

If we talk about the events done by VBI, the word limit would not be enough, in these 5 years VBI has been a part of many event including the fundraisers to raise funds for the education of the children. From being a part of International Yoga Day for 2 consecutive years to having a Health camp for the Needy. VBI also helped Swasthgram Association in their campaign and also had the opportunity to showcase the same at New Delhi. Along with these professional events, VBI never forgot about their children they celebrated every festival with them, from Diwali to Rakshabandhan , From taking them to Church during Christmas to Taking them at an event ‘CHURPY BURPY’ Where the kids participated in a RAMPWALK in front of the audience of Ahmedabad. The smile on the faces of those kids cannot be explained through words, the happiness on their parents face when the kids recite the entire ABC was out of this world, and all the volunteers could experience were the TEARS OF JOY.
