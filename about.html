    • How we started?

    One random day, a group of youngsters belonging to different fields varying from engineering to twelfth grade, from designing to law sat in a room, a thought about taking an action, a step towards the betterment of society. Many resemble the same situation but only few take a step, these youngsters were one of the few who thought of taking an action towards the enhancement of society, helping the government as an NGO to create a better India. On the date, 28 June 2014, VBI (Volunteers for a Bright Inspiration) was created, and it’s going to complete five years of success by this 28 June, 2019 with lot of memories and events. The youngsters have grown and are now on the path of being more experienced in the field. From understanding the reality of poverty and slums to the technical financial management. The group of VBI, has grown and turned to group of more than 150 volunteers, from working at a single location, the volunteers are now close to covering many vast cities of Gujarat.

    what we do?

    Volunteers for a Bright Inspiration works on Eight topics namely: EDUCATION, ENVIORNMENT, DE-ADDICTION, RURAL DEVELOPMENT, HEALTH, WOMEN EMPOWERMENT, SENIOUR CITIZEN AND ANIMAL PROTECTION.The volunteers of VBI, have been giving their weekend to the Nation by educating the people of slum. They’ve taught them from the basics. They’ve inculcated the children with not only basic primary education but also the basic way of living which includes sanitation and well-being. The female volunteers also taught the girls there the importance of being clean and also guided them to stay safe from the people who wish to exploit them. When money was not a problem, the parents of the children used to create a problem saying that they don’t want to send their children. The first 2-3 months were a chaos for VBI but the volunteers stayed determined throughout and got through the problem. They made those children their priority and gave their heart, soul and sweat to make them a better person with regards to education.

    The Volunteers never asked for donation or funds they always used to create a fundraiser event in the form of HYGENIC FOOD STALL so that even the volunteers grow and understand the concept of management and financial aid. During those events the volunteers used to face internal chaos but even that was coped up by them, this shows their maturity level about how they have grown.
